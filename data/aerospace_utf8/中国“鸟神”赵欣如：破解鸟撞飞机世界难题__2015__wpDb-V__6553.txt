ABSTRACT: The network topological model of air systems combat can provide important references for analysis and
design, and improve the air systems combat networks. In order to enhance the validity of the network topological
model of air systems combat and make the model be close to actual combat, combat weapons were divided into differ-
ent node units and the link methods were selected correspondingly based on the composition of the air systems com-
bat. Then the network model was established based on the
theory of complex networks ,and the topological properties
of the networks were analvzed. Simulation results show that the model is effective and feasible.
