YANG Zhe,LI Shu-lin,ZHOU Li, YIN Jun-jie
(Aeronautics and Astronautics Engineering College, Air Force Engineering University, ,Xi「 an Shanxi 710038, China)
ABSTRACT:The decision of application of airborne chaff
centroid jamming can reduce the aircraft hit probability by
radar guided missile and improve the aircraft survivability
in battle. In order to make the model close to actual com-
bat, the model of airborne chaff centroid jamming was established based on the its characteristics, and the movements
of aircraft, chaff and missile were considered by the model. The calculation method on decision of chaff jamming
launching and aircraft tactic maneuver was analyzed based
on the established model. Simulation results show that the
model is effective and feasible.
