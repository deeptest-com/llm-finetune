Feasibility Research on Perpendicular Reentry of Kinetic Energy Payload
Feng Biming, Nie
Wansheng, Li Ke
(Academic of Equipment, Beijing, 101416)
Abstract: Referring to the supposition of ballistic reentry for kinetic energy attacking named as “God’s Rod”, the max
reentry angle of payload controlled by limit thrust and continuous low-thrust was researched using adaptive pseudo-spectral
method, and the feasibility for perpendicular reentry of payload was analyzed. The result illuminated that whatever de-orbit
model was used to ensure the reentry angle of payload close to vertical, the consumption of fuel increased sharply with the
payload mass, the mass ratio of payload to fuel attained 1:9. When a limit thrust was used for de-orbit, the reentry angle could get
close to perpendicular in a short time, but the velocity was only 3.5 km/s. However, when the continuous low-thrust was used,
the reentry angle could be close to vertical with a higher velocity about 7 km/s, but the work time of engine exceeded 1 hour.
Key Words: Kinetic energy; Adaptive pseudo-spectral method; Limit thrust; Continuous low-thrust; Perpendicular reentry

4结“论
本文针对有限推力和连续小推力作用下动能弹垂
直再入的可行性开展了研究分析,得到以下结论:
a)无论采用哪种制动方式,要让动能弹以接近垂
直的倾角再入,必然会消耗大量燃料。以有限推力制
动为例,在接近垂直再入的条件下,动能弹与燃料的
质量比达到1:9。由此可见,“上帝之杖“所描述的动
能打击方式只适合采用小质量动能弹(约100kg),随
着动能弹质量的增加,燃料及相关附加设备的质量将
成倍增加。
b)有限推力制动时,采用大推力发动机可缩短发
动机工作时间和动能弹过渡段飞行时间。但是,这种
制动方式在实现动能弹短时间内以接近垂直倾角再入
的同时,将造成再入速度的损失(再入速度约3.5km/s
左右),影响动能打击的效果。
c)连续小推力制动时,能够保证动能弹在以接近
垂直倾角再入的同时还具有较高的再入速度(7km/s
左右2,但过渡段飞行时间(即发动机工作时间》长达
1h以上,影响再入响应时间,而且对发动机的长时间
工作性能提出了新的要求。
