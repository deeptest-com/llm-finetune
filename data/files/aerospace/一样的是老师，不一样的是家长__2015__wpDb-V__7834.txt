SINS Alignment Algorithm for Marching Vehicles with the Aid of
Distance-Transfer-Unit (DTU)
Chen Hongyue,
Sun Qian, Liu Yuhang
(Beijing Institute of Space Launch Technology, Beijing, 100076)
Abstract: A SINS alignment algorithm for marching vehicles was presented. Alignment process was parted into coarse
alignment and precise alignment. Coarse alignment was achieved in the inertial coordinate system with the aid of
distance-transfer-unit. Precise alignment was achieved through
Kalman filter with ten states. Then the difference between the
velocities obtained respectively from the inertial coordinate system and the DTU was observed. By simulation experiments and
real vehicle tests, alignment for marching vehicles was realized both in the static and swaying base conditions. When driving
route or mode is not limited, SINS achieved an alignment precision same as static base alignment in twenty-five minutes.
Precision of alignment was positively relevant to the time of alignment. The longer the alignment took, the higher the precision
Was.
Key Word: Aid of DTU; Inertial coordinate system; Kalman filtering; Alignment in marching

4结论
本文提出的对准方法可以实现行进间对准,对行
车路线及行车方式不做特殊要求,兼容静基座初始对
准及晃动基座下的初始对准,对准精度与对准的时间
相关,对准时间越长对准精度越高;行车路线及行车
方式也影响对准精度和对准时间。具有行进间对准能
力的车载定位定向设备在出车库前开始进行寻北,武
器系统进入阵地前就实现了定向精度,具备了作战能
力,提高了武器系统的快速反应能力。
Kalman滁波器中噪声矩阵的选择对里程计辅助的
捷联惯导行进间对准精度及对准时间影响较大,进一
步研究噪声矩阵的选择方法或研究自适应Kalman滤
波器可以进一步提高行进闻对准的方位角收敛时间。
